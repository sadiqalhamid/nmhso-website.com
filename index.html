Fostering Academic Excellence

The NMHSO is a Federal Ministry of Education–approved initiative designed to identify, nurture and showcase exceptional talents in medical and health sciences among Nigerian students.

Our Vision

To build a nationally coordinated pipeline of future medical professionals, researchers and health innovators.

Our Mission

To promote excellence, innovation and global competitiveness in medical education through structured competitions.
